HAUNTED MANSION

Two rooms stand between you and the way out. Neither wants you to leave.

Room 1 — Search the drawers for a key. When the light flickers three times, get into the closet or the thing in the dark will take you.

Room 2 — Pull books from the shelf. Good ones give key fragments; bad ones loose a spider. Find all three fragments to move on.

Room 3 — Smash the three cursed mirrors. Each one releases a shadow that hunts you. Break them all, then leave.

Room 4 — The monster wakes and chases. Throw all three levers to open the exit while staying alive. Get caught and it's over.
